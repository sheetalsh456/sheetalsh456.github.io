Winter Research Intern, Indian Institute of Technology, Mumbai [Dec'15]

Worked on the project titled 'Scilab Optimization Toolbox'. I developed optimization modules of fminimax and fgoalattain functions and tools for an open source numerical computation software, Scilab. Fminimax minimizes the worst-case value of a set of multi-variable functions, starting at an initial estimate. Fgoalattain solves the goal attainment problem, which is one formulation for minimizing a multiobjective optimization problem.
